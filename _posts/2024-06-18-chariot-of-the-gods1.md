With our DnD group, horror campaigns are difficult to run. We've spent around two years playing [Curse of Strahd](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Curse_of_Strahd), a signature DnD horror campaign centered on vampire Count Strahd von Zarovich in bleak country of Barovia. Even as our characters lost limbs, whole bodies and any sense of sanity, we as a group were full of jokes and in general marry mood. How could we not be, when Strahd feels like even a bigger emo version of most famous vampire of all times? 

But we had a great time! Were we scared? No. But fun was had, Strahd is dead (or so we think) and it was my turn to GM. And my brilliant idea was another iconic horror, this time in sci-fi environment.
